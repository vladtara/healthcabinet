# Brainstorming Session Results

**Facilitator:** DUDE
**Date:** 2026-03-06

## Session Overview

**Topic:** Personal Health Intelligence SaaS — users upload lab results (photos/PDFs), AI-powered dashboard with visual trend alerts and risk scoring
**Goals:** Product differentiation, MVP scope clarity, monetization angles, risk identification

### Session Setup

Solo founder. MVP in Ukraine, expanding to EU (GDPR). B2C freemium model. No direct competitors identified. Target: individual consumers uploading all lab result types.

## Technique Selection

**Approach:** AI-Recommended Techniques

**Recommended Techniques:**

- **What If Scenarios:** Challenge hidden assumptions, open the solution space wide before narrowing
- **Cross-Pollination:** Steal battle-tested patterns from adjacent industries (Mint, Grammarly, Duolingo, InsideTracker)
- **Reverse Brainstorming:** Stress-test by asking "how could this fail?" — every failure mode becomes a design decision (skipped, user moved to organize)

**AI Rationale:** Healthcare SaaS requires wide exploration first, then proven pattern transfer, then risk hardening — optimal sequence for a solo founder avoiding building the wrong thing.

## Technique Execution Results

### What If Scenarios

**Interactive Focus:** Core product assumptions, AI doctor experience, onboarding flow, upload friction, user relationship with health data

**Key Breakthroughs:**
- The "AI doctor as a relationship" insight — persistent memory makes it irreplaceable
- Onboarding-first baseline dashboard — app feels alive before any data is uploaded
- Proactive test recommendations — shifts product from reactive to proactive health guidance

### Cross-Pollination

**Industries raided:** Personal finance (Mint), developer SaaS (Stripe), document management (Notion), subscription media (Spotify/Calm), consumer tech (Apple ecosystem), legal/doc tech

**Key Breakthroughs:**
- Stripe/Bloomberg aesthetic as positioning statement — serious precision tool, not patient app
- Storage-based upgrade trigger (Dropbox playbook)
- €9.99/month anchored against real doctor visit, not app market

## Idea Organization and Prioritization

### Theme 1: Core Product Foundation *(MVP backbone)*

- **[Feature #8] Magic Universal Upload** — any format, any language, any quality, just works. The entire product trust depends on this being bulletproof and invisible.
- **[Feature #14] Medical Onboarding → Baseline Dashboard** — app knows you before your first upload; shows personalized recommended tests immediately based on age, sex, height, weight, family history.
- **[Feature #20] Universal Health Document Cabinet** — not just labs; all health docs in one place (doctor notes, prescriptions, hospital summaries, vaccination records).
- **[Feature #17] Precision Professional UI** — Bloomberg/Stripe aesthetic, data-dense, elegant, dark mode option. Targets people who take their health seriously.
- **[Feature #13] Full Data Ownership & Deletion** — GDPR-native. Users view, export, and permanently delete all data in one place. "You own your health data" as a genuine marketing message.

### Theme 2: AI Personal Doctor *(Paid tier core value)*

- **[Feature #2] Named Persistent AI Companion** — dedicated AI doctor with full memory of health story, has a name, speaks to you personally.
- **[Feature #7] Persistent Memory & Continuity** — interprets every new upload against your entire history and past conversations.
- **[Feature #18] AI Reasoning Trail** — expandable "based on" section for every insight; shows exactly which data points and context drove the recommendation.
- **[Feature #15] Personalized Test Recommendations** — before any upload, recommends which specific panels to get based on your profile and why.
- **[Feature #3] Voice Health Briefings** — tap to hear AI doctor walk you through new results conversationally.

### Theme 3: Health Intelligence Dashboard *(Free tier differentiator)*

- **[Feature #4] Predictive Health Trajectories** — "at this trend, deficient in ~3 months" not just current values. GPS vs speedometer.
- **[Feature #16] Dynamic Health Calendar** — smart nudges on when to retest based on trends and seasons.
- **[Feature #9] Chronic Condition Focus** — UX optimized for users who test every 3 months for life (diabetes, thyroid, autoimmune, cardiovascular). Highest retention, highest willingness to pay.
- **[Feature #22] Annual Health Year in Review** — beautiful yearly summary of progress, improvements, and AI insights. Retention milestone.

### Theme 4: Business Model

- **[Feature #21] €9.99/month AI Doctor Subscription** — anchored against real doctor visit cost (€50-150+), not app market. Free cabinet + dashboard. Paid AI doctor layer.
- **[Feature #19] Storage-Based Upgrade Trigger** — free tier limited storage; power users naturally hit limit when already hooked.
- **[Feature #10] Family Health Profiles** *(future)* — premium family plan, one account multiple profiles.
- **[Feature #1] Lab Booking Marketplace** *(future)* — direct test booking inside app, labs pay for acquisition.

### Theme 5: Clinical Trust Bridge

- **[Feature #5] Doctor Share Mode** — one-click dashboard export formatted for a physician visit.
- **[Feature #11] Multilingual Health Summary Export** — AI generates plain-language summary in any language. Key for EU expansion and cross-border patients.
- **[Feature #12] Trust-First Design** — transparency baked into UI at every step. Post-MVP priority but shapes architecture.
- **[Feature #6] Lifestyle Context Onboarding** — diet, sleep, activity, family history, symptoms collected upfront; shapes every AI interpretation.
- **[Feature #23] Physician Verification Layer** *(post-MVP)* — licensed doctors verify AI insights, adds clinical credibility.
- **[Feature #1b] Lab Booking Integration** *(future)* — from alert to booked test in 30 seconds.

### Technical Requirements

- **[Technical #1] Invisible Document Intelligence** — bulletproof parsing of any lab format, any language, any country, any quality. Users never see a failed parse. Foundation everything else depends on.

## Hard Constraints (Never Implement)

- No gamification, no scores, no points, no badges
- No engagement streaks or habit mechanics
- No social sharing of health dashboard
- No artificial feature gating on free tier (free tier must be genuinely valuable)

## Session Summary and Insights

**Key Achievements:**
- 23 features + 1 core technical requirement generated
- Clear free vs. paid tier distinction crystallized: cabinet+dashboard (free) vs. AI doctor (paid)
- Strong product positioning identified: precision professional tool, not a patient-facing health app
- Core MVP scope defined: onboarding → upload → dashboard → AI doctor

**Breakthrough Moments:**
- "AI doctor as a relationship" — persistent memory is the churn killer
- Onboarding baseline dashboard — solves cold start problem elegantly
- Chronic condition users as primary target — highest frequency, retention, and willingness to pay
- €9.99 anchored against real doctor visit, not app pricing norms
